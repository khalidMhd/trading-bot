Who is this for?

Traders focused on short-term gold moves (15–30 minutes) who want a systematic approach instead of gut feeling. Whether you trade on MetaTrader 5, cTrader, or another platform, the signals give you a consistent framework to follow or compare against your own analysis.

Data sources

The cloud dashboard uses Yahoo Finance (COMEX Gold GC=F) for live prices. For broker-exact XAUUSD prices, run the bot locally on Windows with MetaTrader 5 connected.

Why we built Trading Bots Live

The retail gold trading world is full of expensive signal groups, black-box "robots," and sellers who show only their winning trades. We found this frustrating and, frankly, harmful to new traders. Our belief is that anyone should be able to access clear, rule-based analysis for free, understand the reasoning behind every signal, and practise thoroughly before risking a single dollar. That belief is the foundation of everything on this site.

How we are different

Most signal services ask you to trust an arrow with no explanation. We do the opposite. Every signal on our dashboard is accompanied by a probability score and a breakdown of the eight technical factors behind it, so you can judge the logic yourself in real time. There is no paywall on the signals, no pressure to deposit with a particular broker, and no promises of guaranteed riches. We would rather earn your trust by being honest than earn a quick subscription with hype. If you want to understand our full methodology, read How It Works, and if you are new to gold entirely, start with our beginner's guide.
