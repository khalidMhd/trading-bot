How to Read XAUUSD BUY/SELL Signals Like a Pro

8 Mar 2026 · 6 min read

Our gold signal dashboard shows more than a simple arrow. Understanding each field helps you filter noise and paper trade with discipline.

Signal state: BUY, SELL, or HOLD

BUY and SELL appear when probability and confluence pass your threshold (default 75%). HOLD means conditions are mixed — the bot is protecting you from low-quality setups.

Probability percentages

You'll see separate BUY and SELL probability scores. Higher numbers mean more indicators align. Don't chase 51% — wait for strong alignment or use paper mode to learn typical ranges.

Entry, stop loss, and take profit

Active signals include three prices based on ATR (volatility):

  • Entry — reference price when the signal fired
  • Stop loss (SL) — invalidation level if the trade goes wrong
  • Take profit (TP) — target based on risk-reward logic

8-factor confluence panel

Each factor (EMA trend, RSI, MACD, ADX, Bollinger Bands, candle flow, M5 confirmation, session liquidity) contributes to the score. Green = supportive, red = against the direction. See full breakdown on How It Works.

Desktop alerts

Click the bell on the dashboard to get notified when a new BUY or SELL fires — useful if you're not watching charts all day.
