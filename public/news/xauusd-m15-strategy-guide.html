XAUUSD M15 Strategy: 15–30 Minute Gold Setups

5 Mar 2026 · 7 min read

The 15-minute (M15) chart is a popular timeframe for gold day traders: fast enough for intraday moves, slow enough to filter random noise. Trading Bots Live is built specifically for this style.

Why M15 for gold?

  • Captures London/NY session moves without H1 lag
  • ATR-based stops fit intraday volatility on XAUUSD
  • M5 confirmation adds timing without over-trading

Core indicators we use

EMA trend defines bias. RSI flags exhaustion. MACD confirms momentum shifts. ADX measures trend strength — weak ADX often means HOLD. Bollinger Bands help spot squeeze breakouts.

Multi-factor scoring vs single indicators

One indicator alone gives false signals. Our bot requires alignment across eight factors before firing BUY/SELL. That reduces whipsaws — at the cost of fewer trades. Quality over quantity.

H1 filter (optional)

Some traders prefer an H1 trend filter. Our cloud deployment runs with H1 filter off for more signals; local MT5 users can enable it in .env for stricter alignment.

Paper trade your edge

Track a week of M15 signals on our dashboard before sizing real positions. Note win rate, average R:R, and which sessions perform best for you.
