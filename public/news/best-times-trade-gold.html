Best Times to Trade Gold (XAUUSD) — Session Guide

10 Mar 2026 · 5 min read

Gold (XAUUSD) does not move equally at all hours. Session overlap, economic releases, and institutional flow create windows where M15 setups are cleaner — and hours where chop eats stop losses.

Why session timing matters for gold

XAUUSD is driven by USD strength, real yields, risk sentiment, and geopolitical headlines. Liquidity clusters when London and New York are both open. Our live signal bot scores session liquidity as one of eight confluence factors.

Asian session (Tokyo / Sydney)

  • Typical window: roughly 00:00–09:00 UTC
  • Character: Often slower ranges; spikes on China/Japan data
  • Tip: Fewer high-probability M15 signals — wait for London open

London session

  • Typical window: roughly 07:00–16:00 UTC
  • Character: Strong breakouts, trend continuation common
  • Tip: Watch the first hour after London open for direction

New York session

  • Typical window: roughly 12:00–21:00 UTC
  • Character: Highest volume when overlapping London (12:00–16:00 UTC)
  • Tip: US CPI, NFP, and FOMC days — widen stops or reduce size

Best overlap for M15 gold trades

The London–New York overlap (about 12:00–16:00 UTC) is widely regarded as the sweet spot for XAUUSD volatility and tight spreads. If you only trade one block per day, start here and paper trade for a week using our dashboard.
