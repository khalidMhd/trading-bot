How to use these calculators

These free tools are designed to be used together as part of a disciplined trading routine. Start with the Position Size Calculator: enter your account balance, the percentage you are willing to risk (1% is a sensible default), and your planned entry and stop-loss prices. The calculator returns the exact lot size that keeps your risk within your limit — removing the guesswork that leads most beginners to over-trade.

Next, use the Risk-to-Reward Calculator to confirm the setup is worth taking. A ratio of 1:1.5 or higher means your potential reward outweighs your risk, which allows you to be profitable even if you win fewer than half your trades. Finally, the Profit & Loss Calculator lets you preview the dollar outcome of a trade at different exit prices, so there are no surprises.

All calculations assume a standard gold contract of 100 ounces per lot, where a $1 move in price equals $100 profit or loss per full lot. Because some brokers use different contract sizes, every calculator lets you adjust this value. Always confirm the exact specification with your own broker.
