Overview

Trading Bots Live ("we", "us", "our") operates https://trading-bots.live. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it when you visit our website.

Information we collect

  • Usage data — pages visited, browser type, device, approximate location (via analytics)
  • Cookies — small files stored by your browser for analytics and advertising
  • Local storage — on the live app (/app), signal history and paper-trade stats are saved in your browser only (not on our servers)

How we use information

  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Measure traffic and popular pages (Google Analytics, when enabled)
  • Display relevant advertisements (ad partners, when enabled)
  • Maintain and secure the service

Third-party services

We may use the following categories of third parties:

  • Analytics — Google Analytics (traffic measurement)
  • Advertising — networks such as PropellerAds, Ezoic, or Google AdSense
  • Hosting — Vercel (website hosting)
  • Market data — Yahoo Finance and similar APIs for gold prices
  • Affiliate partners — broker links (clearly labeled on site)

These providers have their own privacy policies governing their use of data.

Cookies & your choices

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect some site features. When we show a cookie notice, accepting it allows analytics and ad scripts to run.

Children

This site is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Data retention

Server-side API requests are processed in real time and are not stored long-term. Browser local storage on /app persists until you clear your browser data.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Contact us through the website domain for privacy requests.
